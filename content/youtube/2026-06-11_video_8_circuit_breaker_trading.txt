━━━ WHAT IS CIRCUIT BREAKER (2:30 - 4:00) ━━━

[VISUAL: Circuit breaker switch animation]

Circuit breaker — exactly jaisa electrical system mein hota hai.
Jab current zyada ho jaaye — switch off.

Trading mein bhi same concept.

OMNI BRAIN ke 4 circuit breakers:

1. DAILY LOSS LIMIT ────────────────
Account ka 5% loss ho -> Auto halt.
Rest of the day — NO trading.
Agli subah 7 AM -> Auto resume.

2. MAX CONSECUTIVE LOSSES ──────────
3 losses in a row -> Cooling period.
24 hours ka break.
System analyze karega — kya galat hua.

3. DRAW DOWN LIMIT ────────────────
Account 8% neeche -> Emergency stop.
Manual restart required.
Aapko deliberately system chalana padega.

4. VOLATILITY CIRCUIT ──────────────
Market mein abnormal movement -> Auto halt.
Example: NFP announcement mein 200 pips movement.
System pause -> Wait for stability.
