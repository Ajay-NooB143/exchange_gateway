━━━ KELLY FORMULA SIMPLIFIED (2:00 - 4:00) ━━━

[VISUAL: Formula on screen with explanation]

Mathematical formula hai — but simple karke samjhaunga.

KELLY % = (Win Probability × Win/Loss Ratio) - (Loss Probability)

Haan formula dekhne mein complex lagta hai.
Example se samjho:

Maan lo:
• Aapki strategy ka win rate: 60% (0.6)
• Risk:Reward: 1:2 (agar ₹100 risk, toh ₹200 reward)
• Loss probability: 40% (0.4)

Kelly % = (0.6 × 2) - (0.4) / 2
= (1.2 - 0.4) / 2
= 0.8 / 2
= 0.4

Matlab — 40% of your trading capital.

[SHOW: Calculator app doing this calculation]

WAIT — 40% matlab nahi ki pura capital ek trade mein laga do.
Kelly half ya quarter use karte hain.
Conservative version: Kelly / 2 = 20%
Ultra conservative: Kelly / 4 = 10%

OMNI BRAIN uses Kelly / 2 — Half Kelly.
