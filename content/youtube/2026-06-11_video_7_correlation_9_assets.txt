━━━ WHAT IS CORRELATION (0:45 - 2:00) ━━━

[VISUAL: Simple animation showing two lines moving together/opposite]

Correlation — simple definition:
Do assets ke price movement ka relationship.

3 types:

1. POSITIVE CORRELATION (+1.0):
   Dono ek saath upar ya neeche jaate hain.
   Example: SP500 aur NASDAQ

2. NEGATIVE CORRELATION (-1.0):
   Ek upar jaata hai, doosra neeche.
   Example: USD index aur XAUUSD (Gold)

3. ZERO CORRELATION (0.0):
   Koi relationship nahi.
   Example: BTC aur Silver (usually)

[SHOW: Visual examples of each type]

Correlation range: -1.00 to +1.00
• +0.80 to +1.00 — Strong positive
• +0.40 to +0.79 — Moderate positive  
• -0.40 to +0.39 — Weak/None
• -0.79 to -0.40 — Moderate negative
• -1.00 to -0.80 — Strong negative

━━━ PAIRS TRADING STRATEGY (5:30 - 7:00) ━━━

[VISUAL: Pairs trading explained]

Correlation ka sabse powerful use — PAIRS TRADING.

Jab do strongly correlated assets diverge (alag ho jaate hain),
toh wapas converge (ek saath aa) jayenge.

Strategy:

Step 1: Find two strongly correlated assets
Example: EURUSD ↔ GBPUSD (correlation: +0.89)

Step 2: Wait for divergence
EURUSD neeche gaya, GBPUSD upar — abnormal.

Step 3: Trade the convergence
SELL GBPUSD (overperformer)
BUY EURUSD (underperformer)

Step 4: Profit when they converge back

Hedging — opposite position leke risk reduce karna.

Example:

Actually:
