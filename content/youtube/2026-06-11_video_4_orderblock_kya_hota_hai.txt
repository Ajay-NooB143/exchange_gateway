━━━ WHAT IS ORDER BLOCK (0:30 - 2:00) ━━━

[VISUAL: Animated explanation with candlesticks]

Sabse simple definition:
Order Block wo candle hai jahan institutions ne
apna bada order place kiya.

Socho — agar koi bank $10 million ka buy karega,
toh ek candle mein nahi kar sakta.
Price zyada move ho jayega.

Toh kya karte hain?
Market ko manipulate karte hain.
Pehle price neeche le jaate hain (sweep liquidity),
phir wahan se apna order bharte hain.

Wo candle jo price ko wapas upar le aayi —
wo hai BULLISH Order Block.

Iske opposite:
Jab price upar le jaake, sell order bhare —
wo hai BEARISH Order Block.

[VISUAL: Bullish vs Bearish OB side by side]

Bullish OB characteristics:
• Large bearish candle (red)
• Uske baad immediate bullish candle (green)
• Low of OB support zone ban jaata hai

Bearish OB characteristics:
• Large bullish candle (green)
• Uske baad bearish candle (red)
• High of OB resistance ban jaata hai

━━━ 3 TYPES OF ORDER BLOCKS (3:30 - 6:00) ━━━

[VISUAL: Three types illustrated]

TYPE 1: TRENDING OB
Trend ke direction mein banta hai.
Uptrend mein — bullish OB.
Downtrend mein — bearish OB.
Highest probability.

TYPE 2: REVERSAL OB
Market structure break hota hai, phir OB banta hai.
Trend change ka signal.
Zyada patience chahiye.

TYPE 3: INDUCEMENT OB
Liquidity sweep ke baad banta hai.
Fake breakout hota hai, phir price ulta chala jaata hai.
Yeh sabse advanced hai.
