EXPLANATION (8-25 sec):
Fair Value Gap tab banta hai jab price ek direction mein bahut tez jaata hai. Beech ka jo gap hota hai — wo FVG hai.
✅ Bullish FVG — 3 candles ka gap upar ki taraf
✅ Bearish FVG — 3 candles ka gap neeche ki taraf
✅ Price aksar gap fill karne wapas aata hai
